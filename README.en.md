# Gran Maestro

[한국어](README.md) | [English](README.en.md)

> **"I am the Maestro — I conduct, I don't code."**

**You decide. Your AI team delivers.**

Start a conversation with Claude when an idea strikes.
Brainstorm, explore, and shape your plan.
Stack your plans and approve once — Codex and Gemini execute.

## How do you decide?

- **Brainstorm (`/mst:ideation`, `/mst:discussion`)** — Riff on ideas with Claude, Codex, and Gemini. Surface angles, spot tradeoffs, and lock in direction before a single line of code is written.
- **Explore (`/mst:explore`)** — Let AI automatically analyze your codebase and build the evidence your plan needs.
- **Plan (`/mst:plan`)** — Crystallize decisions into plans. Stack as many as you like, then approve them all at once — the whole team moves together.

## Why Gran Maestro?

- **Claude = PM, Codex·Gemini = Dev Team** — Claude writes and reviews specs. Codex and Gemini do the actual implementation inside Git Worktrees.
- **Work continues while you're away** — Approve your specs, step away. Come back to implementation done, review complete, feedback addressed.
- **Real-time dashboard** — Your workflow graph, agent streams, and dependency map — all in one view.
- **Maximize your subscription value** — Claude, Gemini, and Codex you're already paying for, each doing what it does best, working as a team.
